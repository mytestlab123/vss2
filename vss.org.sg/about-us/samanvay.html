Samanvay

We conduct weekly, monthly family get together where families play games, discuss on relevant inspirational and motivational topics and share the parenting experiences.
 
Additional activities of VSS include organizing functions, events seminars, conferences workshops, community services, personality development, yoga, and research work.
 
It is utmost important that the younger generation of today and our future generations born in the Western nations, such as Australia, should know their Hindu cultural background and therefore the need to teach them how to integrate our culture with current lifestyles is as important as ever.
 
Coming to VSS chapter will certainly provide a very useful springboard to achieve this goal.
